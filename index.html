Who We Are

Kindergarten and Childhood is Our Passion

The Kellian School is committed to providing excellence in early childhood education. Our holistic approach combines academic learning with social-emotional development, creating confident, creative, and compassionate learners.

We believe every child deserves a safe, engaging space where they can Explore, Excel, and, Elevate in an atmosphere of warmth and professional care.

Our Activities

Learning With Care, Growing with Confidence

01

Creative Arts & Craft

Children explore colors, textures, and shapes through painting, drawing, and creative projects, fostering self-expression and fine motor skills in a joyful environment.

02

Storytelling & Puppet Show

Imaginative sessions with books, puppets, and role-play that spark curiosity, build language skills, and teach valuable lessons about kindness and friendship.

03

Music & Movement

Singing, dancing help develop rhythm, coordination, and social skills while allowing children to express themselves freely and build confidence.

04

Nature & Garden Club

Hands-on activities like planting seeds, watering plants, and observing insects teach children about the natural world, responsibility, and the joy of nurturing growth.

05

Sensory Play

Engaging stations with sand, water, clay, and other materials stimulate the senses, encourage exploration, and support cognitive and emotional development through play.

06

Building & Construction

Using blocks, LEGO, and other manipulatives, children develop spatial awareness, problem-solving skills, and teamwork as they design and create structures together.
