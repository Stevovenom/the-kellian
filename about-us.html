Who We Are

Learn About Our Work and Cultural Activities

At The Kellian School, we believe early childhood is a critical period for development. Our holistic approach integrates play-based learning, social-emotional development, and academic foundations. We celebrate each child's individuality while fostering a strong sense of community.

With dedicated educators, modern facilities, and proven pedagogical methods, we ensure every child receives the highest quality care and education.

Why Choose Us

Our Core Values

At The Kellian School, we create a warm and supportive kindergarten environment where every child learns, plays, and grows with confidence. Our approach blends guided learning, creativity, and care to nurture each child’s full potential.

Parent & Learner Centric

We design personalized education plans tailored to each child's unique abilities, learning style, and developmental goals, ensuring meaningful progress at their own pace.

Hands on Skills & Independence

Our warm, sensory-friendly spaces create a safe haven where children feel secure, valued, and excited to learn, building confidence and emotional well-being.

Writing Skills & Reading Culture

Through carefully designed play therapy, sensory integration activities, and motor skills development, we support physical, social, and emotional growth.

Character Formation

Our team of qualified special education teachers, therapists, and support staff bring expertise, patience, and genuine care to every child's development journey.

Values - Kindness & Love

We address the whole child—academic skills, communication, social interaction, life skills, and emotional regulation—for comprehensive growth.

Strong Parent Partnership

We work closely with families through regular communication, training, and collaboration, ensuring consistency and support extends from school to home.
